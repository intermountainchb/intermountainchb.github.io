About Us

Established in 2006 as Utah’s only true independent customs brokerage company, Intermountain CHB makes it easier for our clients to move their freight to any location into the United States while staying compliant and avoiding the potential pit falls of international moves and customs related delays. Intermountain CHB is a highly experienced Customs Brokerage company, employing experienced professionals to ensure customers receive quality brokerage service. This experience means that we are intimately familiar with the ever-changing customs regulations. Importers rely on Intermountain CHB for our flexibility, communication, visible ownership, personable staff, and wide range of experience. We provide in-depth expertise on a wide variety of products such as mining machinery, furniture, textiles, sporting goods, rugs, and more.

The list below provides details for specific Customs Brokerage Services offered by Intermountain CHB. If you have any special Customs Brokerage needs not addressed here, please contact us with your request, and we will be happy to assist.

Customs Brokerage Services Include:

  • Harmonized tariff assitance
  • Customs Bond Applications, Placement, and Adjustments
  • Import product line review services
  • Import Security Filing (ISF)
  • Nationally Permitted Customs Broker with Remote Location Filing (RLF) capabilities
  • Other Government Agency (OGA) entry and release – FDA, EPA, FCC
  • In-bond movements between ports or for export
  • Warehouse and Foreign Trade Zone entries and withdrawals
  • Post-entry audit
  • Carnet Clearance Services / Trade Show Coordination
  • EDI capabilities
  • ACE Entry Filer
